Ian Nickels Plastering

07790 273725

Ian Nickels is a professional plasterer with over 35 years’ experience, who after serving his apprenticeship decided to set up business on his own. Throughout that time the business has developed thanks to an extremely high standard of workmanship and first class customer service.

Based in Swindon, Wiltshire, Ian offers a professional plastering service throughout Witshire and the neighbouring counties. Plastering services include, plasterboard installation, dry lining, skimming, coving, rendering, lime plastering and tiling.

Ian will meet all your plastering requirements, domestic or commercial, large or small. He is fully insured, qualified to City and Guilds standards and offers a free, no obligation estimation service.
